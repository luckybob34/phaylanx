# SOW Template

> Required sections and writing rules for Statement of Work generation.

---

## Required Sections (in order)

### 1. Header Block
Project title, client name, prepared by, date, version number.

### 2. Background and Purpose
Why this engagement exists. Client's business need. Current state.

### 3. Scope of Work
Narrative description + **In Scope** list + **Out of Scope** list.

### 4. Deliverables
Table: ID (D1, D2...) | Name | Description | Acceptance criteria | Due date.

### 5. Timeline and Milestones
Start/end dates. Milestone table mapped to deliverables. Dependencies noted.

### 6. Assumptions and Dependencies
What must be true. Client responsibilities. Third-party dependencies.

### 7. Staffing and Roles
Table: Role | Responsibilities | Allocation (% or hours/week).

### 8. Pricing
Pricing model. Total cost. Breakdown by phase. Payment schedule. Expense policy.

### 9. Risk Register
Table: Risk ID | Description | Probability | Impact | Mitigation.

### 10. Change Management
How changes are requested. Impact assessment. Approval process. Effect on pricing/timeline.

### 11. Acceptance Criteria and Process
Review period (5 business days). What constitutes acceptance. Dispute resolution.

### 12. Signatures
Provider and client signature blocks.

---

## Writing Rules

1. **Be specific** — "Deliver architecture document" not "provide documentation"
2. **Be measurable** — "3 sprint cycles of 2 weeks each" not "several weeks"
3. **Define terms** — If you use "production-ready," say what that means
4. **Avoid jargon** — The signer may not be technical
5. **One deliverable = one line** — Don't bundle
6. **Tie everything together** — Deliverables → milestones → pricing → acceptance
7. **Protect margins** — Out-of-scope section exists for a reason
8. **Version control** — Always include version number
